Academics at Indian Institute

At Indian Institute of Technology, we offer a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs to cater to diverse academic interests. Our academic structure is designed to foster innovation, critical thinking, and excellence in all fields of study.

Bachelor’s Degrees

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Mechanical Engineering

Master’s Degrees

  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Computer Science
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Civil Engineering

Research Opportunities

Indian Institute offers various research opportunities in collaboration with industry leaders. Our faculty members are engaged in cutting-edge research in diverse fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Robotics, and more.
