Admissions at Indian Institute of Technology

Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) offers an exciting opportunity for aspiring students to pursue world-class education in engineering and technology. Our rigorous admission process ensures that we bring in students with exceptional academic talent and a passion for innovation.

Admission Process

1. Submit your online application form.
2. Appear for the entrance exam (if applicable).
3. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview.
4. Final selection based on academic performance and interview.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have passed 12th grade with a minimum of 60% marks.
  • Must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
  • Age limit: Must be under 25 years of age at the time of admission.
